Our project offers a unique beacon-based solution to prevent separation and loss of personal belongings. Unlike traditional trackers, it operates without requiring mobile internet data, resulting in cost savings. The device is versatile, easily attachable to various items, and provides dual functionality by also acting as a tracker, notifying users when they are near a lost item. With scalability in mind, the project allows for continuous improvement and adaptation to meet evolving needs. It's a reliable and efficient way to safeguard valuables, prevent loss, and provide peace of mind.

1. Beacon to Beacon:
   - This design involves multiple beacons communicating with each other to determine the distance between them.
   - Each beacon would emit a Wi-Fi signal that can be detected by nearby beacons.
   - When two beacons are separated by a certain distance, they can send signals to each other to trigger the vibrating and buzzing alerts.
   - This design allows for a network of beacons to be set up, creating a broader coverage area.

2. Beacon to Mobile Phone:
   - In this design, the beacon communicates directly with a mobile phone via a Wi-Fi connection or a dedicated mobile app.
   - The beacon would emit a signal that can be detected by the mobile phone when they are in proximity to each other.
   - When the beacon and the phone are separated beyond a specific distance, the phone can receive a signal from the beacon and trigger the alerts, such as vibrating and buzzing.

The project has several unique features:

1. Cost-Effective Solution: Unlike many other tracking devices, this project does not rely on mobile internet data, resulting in cost savings for users.

2. Dual Functionality: In addition to preventing separation and loss, the device can also act as a tracker. It notifies the owner when they are in close proximity to a lost device by varying the intensity of the buzzer and vibration based on the nearness.

3. Versatile Attachment Options: The device is designed to be easily attachable and detachable. It can be secured to various items such as wristwatches, belts, keys, phones, and more, providing flexibility and convenience for users.

4. Scalability and Continuous Improvement: The project has a scalable nature, allowing for future enhancements and improvements. This ensures that the device can evolve and adapt to meet changing needs and technological advancements over time.
