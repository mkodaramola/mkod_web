About Me.

I am Oluwafemi Michael Daramola, a passionate Embedded Systems and Robotics Engineer with expertise in hardware and software development. I have strong skills in embedded systems, robotics, automation, and programming languages including Embedded C, C++, Python, MATLAB, and Flask framework. Currently based in Ibadan, Nigeria.

I hold a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from the Federal University of Technology, Akure (2019-2024) with a CGPA of 4.34/5.00. My thesis focused on the simulation of a 3-phase Brushless DC Motor Control for industrial applications.

I have industrial and research experience as a Development Engineer intern, where I worked on motor control systems, sensor design, and microcontroller programming. I am also a team leader for CanSat FUTA, leading miniaturized satellite development projects.

Beyond my technical skills, I am a devoted Christian and an enthusiastic mentor and trainer in embedded systems, robotics, and IoT.
