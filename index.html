About Me.

I am Oluwafemi Michael Daramola, a Graduate Member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE) and an Embedded Systems, Power Electronics, and Robotics Engineer with hands-on experience across renewable energy systems, motor control, IoT, and applied software. I work comfortably across hardware design, firmware, PCB development, and the Python tooling that supports testing, telemetry, and automation. Currently based in Ibadan, Nigeria.

I hold a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from the Federal University of Technology, Akure (2019-2024) with a CGPA of 4.34/5.00. My thesis focused on the simulation of a 3-phase Brushless DC Motor Control for industrial applications.

I have industrial and research experience spanning high frequency inverter design, battery management systems, C2000-based motor control firmware, sensor electronics, and embedded communications. As CanSat FUTA team lead, I coordinated miniaturised satellite development, telemetry software, design reviews, and cross-functional delivery.

Beyond engineering delivery, I enjoy mentoring, technical training, and research communication. My work has also led to publications in BLDC motor control and CanSat development, alongside memberships in Black in Robotics and Black in AI.
