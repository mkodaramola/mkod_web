Additional Product & Hardware Concepts

Three More Projects from My Archive.

These projects extend the portfolio into energy harvesting, smart-home safety, and health-focused item tracking. Each one combines practical hardware thinking with system design, prototyping, and user-centered problem solving.

Energy Harvesting

PowerTread

A Team AUTO-X concept focused on harvesting usable electrical output from transducers through rectification and capacitor-boost design. The documented work covers circuit design, soldering, multi-transducer setup, and voltage-output testing.

Project Poster Presentation Deck Prototype Photo

Smart Home Safety

SafeSwitch

A smart home automation and safety device built to reduce energy waste and the risks tied to unattended appliances. It combines scheduled switching, sensor-based usage awareness, remote control, contactless interaction, and speech-recognition support.

Project Notes Embedded C Code Presentation Deck

Health + Anti-Loss Tracking

Wi-Stick

A beacon-based device designed to prevent separation from valuable or health-critical items, especially asthma inhalers. Wi-Stick uses Wi-Fi and beacon communication for low-cost proximity alerts, tracker behavior, and broader beacon-to-beacon coverage.

Project Summary Use-Case Analysis Presentation Deck

Acelife Healthtech

Smart Posture Monitoring Wearable

A wearable posture correction system built around an ESP32 and MPU9250, with BLE telemetry, threshold configuration, vibration feedback, and posture direction tracking for real-time user guidance.
